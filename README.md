## Motivation:
As internet becomes more and more infused into the modern world, many locations in appalachia have yet to be brought to the modern standard. Many locations seemingly struggle to keep up with the breathtaking pace of technology. When we were provided with this census data as part of the Insights Team for Generation Newforce, a subsidiary of Generation West Virginia, I wanted to focus on problems related to access to this newer human right.

## Questions:
* Is there a trend between unemployment and internet access?
* What counties have the worst access?
* Are there outliers based on geographical criteria?
